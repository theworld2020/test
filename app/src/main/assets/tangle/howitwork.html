HOW DOES TANGLE MASTER 3D WORK?

Tangle Master 3D is a surprisingly simple experience that challenges you to untie a series of knots. Untangle is also used loosely here, as many of the ropes aren’t, in fact, all that much tangled at all. They’re often just crossed over, which counts in Tangle Master 3D’s eyes.

Manage to untangle or, at the very least, uncross the ropes, and you can progress to the next level, receiving a shower of coins for your trouble, which you can spend on new cosmetics. These include new rope, nail, and pole designs.
