HOW TO MAKE MONEY – TIPS, TRICKS, AND CHEATS

Now that you’ve got a good understanding of how to untangle knots, here are a bunch of tips and tricks to help you make the biggest income.

WATCH AN AD BETWEEN EVERY LEVEL

Each time you complete a level, you get the opportunity to triple your earnings by watching a short ad. You will earn a lot more cash, faster, by watching ads between each level, so we thoroughly recommend doing this every time.

Open  every  chest

Each time you complete a series of levels, you’ll get the opportunity to open one of three locked chests, and you’ll get a free key. These chests can include a free cosmetic item, which will save you a whopping 2,000 coins, so it’s totally worth opening all three of them, and you can do so by watching ads to get free keys.

YOU CAN GET 600 FREE COINS INTERMITTENTLY BY WATCHING AN AD

We’re not sure exactly how often you can do this, but you can watch an ad intermittently from the cosmetic purchase screen to get your hands on 600 free coins. Check back at least daily to see if you can watch a new ad.
